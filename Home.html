PSG are Ligue 1 Champions

PSG claimed the Ligue 1 title on Saturday night, thanks to a 1-1 draw with Lens. Lionel Messi put them in the lead with a thunderbolt 15 minutes from time, but lens shocked the home supporters with an equaliser 5 minutes from time by Corentin Jean.
